# Vrom Platform FAQ & Knowledge Base

## Wallet & Payments
### How do I deposit money?
To deposit money, go to the "Wallet" section and select "Deposit". Enter the amount and follow the Paystack checkout instructions. Your wallet will be updated instantly after a successful payment.

### How do I withdraw funds?
Withdrawals are currently processed via M-Pesa. Go to "Wallet" -> "Withdraw", enter the amount, and it will be sent to your registered phone number.

### What is Escrow?
Escrow is our secure payment system. When you order a product or book a ride, the funds are "locked" in your wallet. They are only released to the seller or rider once you confirm delivery or completion via the OTP or GPS verification.

## Rider Requirements
### How do I become a rider?
To become a rider, you must upload a clear selfie, your ID (Front & Back), a photo of your vehicle, and a valid "Certificate of Good Conduct". Once uploaded, our admin team will review your account within 24 hours.

### What are the rider earnings?
Riders earn 80% of every trip fare. Vrom takes a 20% commission to maintain the platform and insurance.

## Orders & Deliveries
### How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time on the map once a rider has picked it up from the seller.

### What if my order is wrong?
If the order is incorrect, do not provide the OTP to the rider. Contact support immediately through the chat.
